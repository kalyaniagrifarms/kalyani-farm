Biodiversity & Sustainability

At Kalyani Farms, biodiversity is part of everyday farming life.

कल्याणी फार्ममध्ये जैवविविधता ही रोजच्या शेतीचा अविभाज्य भाग आहे.

Biodiversity on Our Farm

Our farm supports birds, bees, animals, native plants, and mixed crops.

Sustainable Practices

No chemical inputs, seasonal harvest, traditional processing.
